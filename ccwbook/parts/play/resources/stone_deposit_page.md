# Stone Deposit

Stone deposits are the main source of stone in the game. They are the most common deposits in the game and can be found in all locations. They have a grey color and a rough texture.

## Drop on Hit

- **Stone Chunk** 100%
- **Coal Chunk** 50%

## Drop on Destroy

- **Coal Chunk** 100%
- **Amethyst** 20%
