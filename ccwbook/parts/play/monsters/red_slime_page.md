# Blue Slime

The red slimes are small and fast creatures. They are weak alone, but they can be dangerous in groups. They are protective and will attack pets if they are too close or if you attack one of them.

## Stats

- **Rank:** 1
- **Health:** 12
- **Speed:** 12

    - **Range**: 3
